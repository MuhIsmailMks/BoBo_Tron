Connect Your Wallet

You’ll need a Tron wallet to interact with TronSwap. Common wallets include TronLink and Trust Wallet. Click on the wallet icon and follow the instructions to connect your wallet to TronSwap.

Select the BoBo tron

Choose the BoBo tron you want to buy. TronSwap will show a list of available BoBo tron. You can either select from the list or enter the BoBo tron's contract address if it’s not listed.

Enter Amount

Enter the amount of the token you want to purchase. TronSwap will automatically show you the amount you’ll receive and the estimated fees.

Confirm and Swap

Review the details of your transaction. If everything looks correct, click the “Swap” button. You might need to confirm the transaction in your wallet.
